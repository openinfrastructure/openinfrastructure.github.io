About

Jeff McCune

  • 2006 - 2010

    Our Humble Beginnings

    While studying Computer Science at Ohio State Jeff worked full time as a systems administrator for the Department of Mathematics. He adopted Puppet and solved nearly all by rebooting holding the "N" key to netboot and automatically configure everything the faculty and grad students needed to do their work.

  • 2010 - 2015

    The Growth of Puppet

    In 2010 Jeff joined Puppet as an early employee. He traveled the world helping large enterprises automate their datacenters. Jeff was also a core contributor and software developer working with the Puppet open source community on Puppet core and Puppet server.

  • January 2012

    Pro Puppet Published

    Jeff is the co-author of Pro Puppet, which is a best-selling book helping thousands of people get up and running with Puppet. In particular, Jeff focused on scalable architecture with Puppet documenting how to manage many tens of thousands of machines.

  • October 2015

    Open Infrastructure Services

    In October of 2015 Jeff founded Open Infrastructure Services with the goal of helping clients get the most benefit from their investment in technology and automation.
